Research Areas

We explore black holes, exoplanets, and cosmology to redefine our understanding of the cosmos.

  • Black Holes: Simulating event horizons and accretion physics
  • Cosmology: Exploring dark energy and cosmic inflation
  • Exoplanets: Analyzing biosignatures in alien atmospheres
  • Gravitational Waves: Studying neutron star and black hole mergers
  • Quantum Time: Investigating multiverse and time-loop theories
